Neha Mehta (Anjali Mehta)

Neha Mehta is an Indian actress who mainly works in Hindi
television. Mehta is best known for her portrayal of Anjali
Taarak Mehta in India's longest-running sitcom, Taarak
Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashmah. She has also played the lead
role of Vaishali in Dollar Bahu and Saroj Chopra in Bhabhi. Wikipedia
Born: 9 June 1978 (age 46 years), Bhavnagar
Height: 1.68 m
